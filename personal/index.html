A quiet place to watch Nguyen's money.

Getting your data ready takes three steps in your OneDrive Nemla folder.

Step 1 · Statements
Drop each month's bank or card CSV into
Apps / Personal / YYYY / MM /
For example, the April 2026 ANZ credit statement goes into Apps/Personal/2026/04/anz-credit.csv.
Step 2 · Categorisation rules (optional)
Bulk teach the dashboard which patterns map to which categories by saving
Apps / Personal / rules.csv
Two columns: Pattern,Category. Patterns match case insensitive substrings of the transaction description.
Step 3 · View & export
Once signed in, pick a month from the top bar. Use the Export PDF button in the filters bar to download a snapshot of the dashboard for distribution.
