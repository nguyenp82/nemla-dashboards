One quick setup, then you're sorted.

Drop your business CSVs into OneDrive, then sign in with Microsoft to load your dashboard.

1
Create the folder structure
In your Nemla OneDrive, the app uses: OneDrive / Apps / Business / 2026 / 03
Folders are YYYY for year and MM for month (zero-padded). Create as many months as you need.
2
Drop your CSVs each month
Goes into the monthly folder: OneDrive / Apps / Business / 2026 / 03 / cba-bank.csv
Main bank and both credit cards for that month. Any filename works. CBA, ANZ, and most AU banks are auto-detected.
3
Pick month or quarter view
After sign in, toggle between Month and Quarter at the top of the dashboard.
Quarter view aggregates three months at once and is ideal for BAS lodgement prep.
